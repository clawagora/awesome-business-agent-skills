# Awesome Business Agent Skills [![Awesome](https://awesome.re/badge.svg)](https://awesome.re)

> A curated list of AI agent skills, templates, and resources to help business owners automate operations, customer support, marketing, and more.

AI agents are transforming how businesses operate. But finding the right skills — the modular capabilities that make agents actually useful — is hard. This list cuts through the noise and highlights **production-ready skills** organized by business function.

Whether you run an e-commerce store, a consulting firm, or a SaaS company, these skills can help you automate the repetitive work and focus on what matters.

## Platforms

## Email & Communication

Skills for managing inboxes, drafting responses, and keeping communication flowing.

- **Email Triage & Auto-Response** - Classify incoming emails by urgency and intent, draft contextual replies, and escalate when needed. Works with Gmail and Outlook. `openclaw`
- **Meeting Scheduler** - Parse scheduling requests from emails and messages, check calendar availability, and propose meeting times. Integrates with Google Calendar and Outlook. `openclaw` `hermes`
- **Email Newsletter Composer** - Generate weekly/monthly newsletters from your notes and business updates. Exports to Mailchimp, Substack, or raw HTML. `openclaw`
- **Slack Channel Summarizer** - Digest busy Slack channels into daily or weekly summaries with action items highlighted. `openclaw`
- **Multi-Channel Inbox Unifier** - Aggregate messages from Gmail, Telegram, Slack, and WhatsApp into a single prioritized queue for review. `openclaw`

## Customer Support

Skills for handling tickets, answering FAQs, and keeping customers happy.

- **FAQ Knowledge Base Agent** - Ingest your help docs and product pages, then answer customer questions accurately with source citations. Knows when to escalate to a human. `openclaw` `hermes`
- **Ticket Classification & Routing** - Auto-tag incoming support tickets by category, sentiment, and priority, then route to the right team member. `openclaw`
- **Customer Sentiment Tracker** - Analyze support conversations and reviews to surface recurring pain points and satisfaction trends. `openclaw`
- **Return & Refund Processor** - Walk customers through return eligibility, generate return labels, and process refund requests following your policy rules. `openclaw`
- **Onboarding Guide** - Deliver personalized onboarding sequences to new customers based on their plan, industry, and stated goals. `openclaw` `hermes`

## Sales & CRM

Skills for prospecting, qualifying leads, and managing your pipeline.

- **Lead Qualifier** - Score inbound leads based on your ICP criteria (company size, industry, budget signals) and enrich with public data. `openclaw`
- **Follow-Up Drip Composer** - Generate personalized follow-up email sequences based on prospect behavior and conversation history. `openclaw`
- **CRM Data Hygiene** - Detect duplicate contacts, fill in missing fields, and flag stale deals in your pipeline. Works with HubSpot, Salesforce, and Pipedrive. `openclaw`
- **Proposal Generator** - Draft customized proposals and SOWs from your templates, pulling in client details and project scope automatically. `openclaw`
- **Competitive Intel Monitor** - Track competitor pricing pages, blog posts, and social mentions. Deliver weekly briefings on market moves. `openclaw`

## Content & Marketing

Skills for creating, scheduling, and analyzing content.

- **Blog Post Drafter** - Generate long-form blog posts from outlines or bullet points, matching your brand voice and SEO guidelines. `openclaw` `hermes`
- **Social Media Scheduler** - Create platform-optimized posts (LinkedIn, Twitter/X, Instagram captions) from a single content brief and queue them for posting. `openclaw`
- **SEO Audit & Recommendations** - Crawl your site pages and generate actionable SEO improvement reports covering meta tags, content gaps, and internal linking. `openclaw`
- **Press Release Writer** - Draft press releases following AP style from your product launch notes or company announcements. `openclaw` `hermes`
- **Content Repurposer** - Transform a single piece of content (blog post, webinar transcript, podcast notes) into multiple formats: social posts, email snippets, infographics copy. `openclaw`

## Operations & Admin

Skills for keeping the business running smoothly day-to-day.

- **Chief of Staff Agent** - Manage your calendar, prepare meeting agendas, capture action items, and send follow-up summaries. Integrates with Google Calendar, Slack, and Google Docs. `openclaw`
- **SOP Documentation Builder** - Convert screen recordings, chat logs, or verbal instructions into structured Standard Operating Procedures. `openclaw` `hermes`
- **Vendor & Contract Tracker** - Track contract renewal dates, payment terms, and vendor performance. Send alerts before deadlines. `openclaw`
- **Project Status Reporter** - Aggregate updates from Jira, Asana, or Trello and generate weekly status reports for stakeholders. `openclaw`
- **Document Organizer** - Auto-classify and tag documents in Google Drive or Dropbox based on content, date, and project context. `openclaw`

## Finance & Accounting

Skills for managing money, invoices, and financial reporting.

- **Invoice Processor** - Extract line items, amounts, and dates from incoming invoices (PDF, email). Match against purchase orders and flag discrepancies. `openclaw`
- **Expense Categorizer** - Classify bank and credit card transactions into accounting categories. Export to QuickBooks, Xero, or CSV. `openclaw` `hermes`
- **Cash Flow Forecaster** - Project 30/60/90-day cash flow based on receivables, payables, and historical patterns. `openclaw`
- **Financial Report Generator** - Compile monthly P&L, balance sheet summaries, and KPI dashboards from your accounting data. `openclaw`
- **Tax Document Organizer** - Gather and categorize receipts, 1099s, and deductible expenses for tax preparation. `openclaw` `hermes`

## HR & Recruiting

Skills for hiring, onboarding, and managing your team.

- **Resume Screener** - Score applicants against your job requirements, flag standout candidates, and generate shortlist summaries. `openclaw`
- **Interview Scheduler** - Coordinate interview times across multiple interviewers and candidates, handling timezone differences. `openclaw` `hermes`
- **Job Description Writer** - Generate inclusive, SEO-optimized job postings from your role requirements and company culture notes. `openclaw` `hermes`
- **Employee FAQ Bot** - Answer common HR questions (PTO policy, benefits, expense procedures) from your internal handbook. `openclaw` `hermes`
- **Onboarding Checklist Manager** - Track new hire onboarding tasks, send reminders, and report completion status to managers. `openclaw`

## Legal & Compliance

Skills for reducing legal overhead and staying compliant.

- **Contract Reviewer** - Highlight non-standard clauses, missing terms, and potential risks in vendor and client contracts. `openclaw`
- **Privacy Policy Generator** - Draft GDPR/CCPA-compliant privacy policies based on your data collection practices. `openclaw` `hermes`
- **Compliance Checklist Runner** - Walk through industry-specific compliance checklists (SOC 2, HIPAA, PCI-DSS) and track completion. `openclaw`
- **NDA Drafter** - Generate mutual or one-way NDAs from your templates with party details auto-filled. `openclaw` `hermes`
- **Regulatory Change Monitor** - Track regulatory updates in your industry and summarize how changes may affect your business. `openclaw`

## E-Commerce

Skills for online stores and product businesses.

- **Product Description Writer** - Generate compelling product descriptions optimized for search from your specs and photos. `openclaw` `hermes`
- **Inventory Alert Manager** - Monitor stock levels and send reorder alerts when inventory drops below thresholds. `openclaw`
- **Review Response Drafter** - Compose professional responses to customer reviews (positive and negative) matching your brand tone. `openclaw` `hermes`
- **Abandoned Cart Recovery** - Generate personalized recovery emails based on cart contents, customer history, and timing. `openclaw`
- **Pricing Competitor Tracker** - Monitor competitor product prices and alert you to significant changes in your market. `openclaw`

## Data & Analytics

Skills for making sense of your business data.

- **Report Summarizer** - Digest CSV exports, dashboards, or analytics reports into plain-English executive summaries. `openclaw` `hermes`
- **Survey Analyzer** - Process survey responses (NPS, CSAT, open-ended) and extract themes, trends, and recommended actions. `openclaw`
- **KPI Dashboard Narrator** - Transform your weekly KPIs into a written narrative explaining what moved, why, and what to watch. `openclaw`
- **Churn Risk Detector** - Analyze customer usage and engagement patterns to flag accounts at risk of churning. `openclaw`
- **Market Research Compiler** - Aggregate data from multiple sources into structured market research briefs for strategic decisions. `openclaw`
